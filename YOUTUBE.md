## Description
Silver Passport turns each piece of physical bullion into a verifiable on-chain custody passport on Sui, with every coin photo and the AI auditor memory stored on Walrus.

Three ideas make it more than a token mint:
- Verified-seller gate: minting is gated on-chain. An unverified wallet cannot mint - the Move contract aborts with ENotVerified. Sellers apply with EIN / W-9, get approved on-chain, and only then can they mint.
- Deterministic AI reserve auditor: every figure is computed straight from chain state - the model only narrates, it cannot invent a number. Each attestation is written back to Walrus and becomes the agent memory.
- Real marketplace: gold and silver priced off live spot, with a Sui Kiosk TransferPolicy that will not settle a sale until a 1 percent royalty is paid on-chain. Buyers sign in-browser with Slush.

Honest framing: this is witnessed attestation that a verified party held a specific unit and locked its evidence at a recorded moment - not trustless proof of current physical possession. Sui testnet.
