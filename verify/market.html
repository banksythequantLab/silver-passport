How the custody loop works
01 · VERIFY
Sellers prove EIN / W-9; the operator approves them on-chain. Unverified wallets can't mint.
02 · INTAKE
A verified seller takes the metal into custody and mints its passport — the photo locks onto Walrus.
03 · ATTEST
The metal is held and insured; the AI auditor re-attests the whole reserve back to Walrus.
04 · TRADE
Buyers move between crypto and metal; every resale pays a 1% royalty on-chain.
05 · REDEEM
Burn the passport and the seller ships the physical metal — the claim is extinguished on delivery.
